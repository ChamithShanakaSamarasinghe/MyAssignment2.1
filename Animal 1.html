Activity 1: Play time with the Elephants

Say hello to the Elephants

All of our visitors are cordially welcomed to participate in one of our zoo's proudly arranged programs. Visit the elephant fields and get up up face to face with the elephants. For example, you can pet them, feed them, bathe them, and ride an elephant around the zoo while having a good time. The children can also interact with the baby elephants by feeding them. The elephant attendants will, of course, keep an eye on the whole thing.


This activity is timed at 16 hours and 30 minutes in the evening.

The cost for this activity is as follows

Foriegners -> 3000 rupees
