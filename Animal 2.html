Activity 2: Show time with the Sea Lions on water.

Meet the Sea lions

The Sea Lion show starts at 10:30 in the morning where the Sera Lion caretakers host the show with the cute Sea Lions to entertain the audience.

After the show, the audience can get right up close with a sea lion while engaging with them and asking behaviors from afar. It's a fantastic way for non-swimmers or those who prefer not to get wet to engage with a sea lion.

If the spectators like, they may take photographs with the sea lions and infant sea lions in order to capture the joy of encountering a sea lion, which is, obviously, open to the public.
