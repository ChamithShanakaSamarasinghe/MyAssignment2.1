What kinds of animals are in the zoo?

Different types of animals!!

  • Bengal Tigers, both orange and white, could be seen here. The Sri Lankan leopard, also known as the "Kotiya" or "Diviy" in Sinhala, is a subspecies of leopard that is native to Sri Lanka and the country's top predator. Small indigenous cats such as the fishing cat (Prionailurus viverrinus), the Rusty spotted cat (Prionailurus rubiginosus phillipsi), and the Jungle cat (Felis chaus kelaarti) can be found throughout the country and in the zoo.

However, it was later discovered that she was suffering from digestive issues. Rosi's caretaker, Dr. Samanthi Mendis, stated the animal was regularly unwell. Information regarding some of the plants in the zoo.

Name Most Prominent Feature Benefits Images

Shrubs

 Broad leave plants They offer shade, increase soil stability, improve air quality, and give wildlife habitat

Ferns

 The frond that comprises of two primary parts: the stipe and the blade, is the most visible portion of ferns. It enhances humidity by naturally restoring moisture to the air and helps to clear the home of dangerous contaminants.

Herbs

 They are small plants with a soft and delicate stem. They also work well as container plants, making it simple to keep them where they can be easily snipped for dinner or a bouquet.
